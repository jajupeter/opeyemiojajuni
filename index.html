About me

I am a post-doctoral research scholar with over five years of multidisciplinary expertise at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), cybersecurity, and immersive spatial computing. As lead researcher in the Virtual Reality (VR) Jags Computer Automatic Virtual Environment (CAVE) Laboratory – XR & AI Learning Innovation Research Group , I have driven $10M+ in federally funded projects ( NSF-IUSE, SURADD, ICLERT) under the guidance of Dr. Albertha Lawson, Dr. Patrick Mensah, and Dr. Michael Stubblefield. Prior to my doctorate, I engineered secure, high-availability enterprise networks with 99.99%+ uptime, led cross-functional deployments of campus-wide wireless and IP surveillance systems, and drove performance gains of 100% in speed, 60% in security, and 50% in cost savings through virtualization and cloud migration.

During my doctorate, I pioneered high-fidelity CAVE VR systems to advance computational thinking, delivering immersive and collaborative learning experiences that redefine visual realism, interactivity, and personalization. By integrating machine learning with advanced statistical modeling, I create predictive and adaptive insights that address critical cognitive skill gaps, enabling XR applications to dynamically learn from and respond to users in real time. This fusion of innovation, scalability, and human-centered design is catalyzing transformative change across STEM education, industrial training, and next-generation computing ecosystems.

Research Interest

My work center around creating high-fidelity, AI-driven immersive systems that seamlessly integrate into learning, work, and everyday life to enhance human-machine interaction. I fuse rigorous scientific methodology with real-world deployment to deliver scalable, high-impact XR solutions that accelerate workforce readiness, drive workforce upskilling, and enhancing human-machine interaction.

My current research interest

  • Generative AI for adaptive XR environments that personalize content and interaction based on user behavior and learning needs.

  • Machine Learning algorithm cognitive assessment and personalized learning pathways.

  • AI-driven immersive collaboration systems for real-time co-creation, data visualization, simulation, design, and prototying.

  • User experience (UX) research to create immersive, user-centric experiences that enhance skills acquisition and adaptability in next-gen technology workforces.

  • High-fidelity immersive experiences optimized for next-gen hardware with retina-quality resolution and ultra-low latency.

  • Reality capture and digital twin development for enterprise and educational applications using LiDAR and 3D scanning.

  • Human-centered, privacy-preserving AI for real-time cognitive assessment and adaptive feedback in XR environments.
